Biography

Hi there 👋, I am a PhD student at the University of Helsinki 🇫🇮 under the supervision of Luigi Acerbi and Samuel Kaski. My research is funded by the Finnish Doctoral Program Network in Artificial Intelligence and focuses on probabilistic machine learning and amortized Bayesian inference.

Previously, I obtained my Master’s degree from the Eindhoven University of Technology (TU/e) 🇳🇱 and my Bachelor’s degree from Nankai University 🇨🇳, both in Computer Science.
